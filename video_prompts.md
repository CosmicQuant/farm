# Professional Video Generation Prompts (Veo 3 / High-End AI)

Use these prompts when generating your video. You can attach your existing video clips as "image/video references" layout to guide the AI, while using these text prompts to define the style and finish.

## Option 1: The "International Estate" Look (Grand & Cinematic)
**Best for:** Hero section background, showcasing scale.
> **Prompt:** Cinematic wide-angle drone shot of a high-end commercial feedlot in Kenya during golden hour. Lush green fields of tall Napier grass swaying gently in the wind. Healthy, well-fed Dorper sheep and Boer goats grazing peacefully in modern, clean enclosures. The Tana River glistening in the background. Warm sunlight, 4k resolution, slow smooth camera movement, high production value, BBC Earth documentary style, photorealistic, sharp focus.

## Option 2: The "Premium Quality" Detail (Slow Mo)
**Best for:** Livestock section, showcasing animal health.
> **Prompt:** Extreme close-up slow-motion shot of a prize-winning Boer goat eating fresh green Napier grass. The animal is muscular and healthy with a shiny coat. Soft bokeh background of a modern farm fence. Natural sunlight illuminating the texture of the grass and the animal's fur. High fashion agricultural photography, depth of field, vibrant colors, crystal clear 1080p, commercial grade.

## Option 3: The "Abundance & Harvest" Vibe
**Best for:** Crops section, showing fertility.
> **Prompt:** Low angle tracking shot moving through dense, tall, vibrant green Napier grass. The grass is thick and lush, signifying abundance. In the distance, a modern irrigation system is visible. The scene feels wealthy and well-managed. Bright blue sky, sharp contrast, highly detailed textures, professional agricultural commercial style.

## Option 4: The "Bustling Activity" (Time-lapse/Speed)
**Best for:** About section, showing efficiency.
> **Prompt:** Timelapse of a busy, professional farm feedlot. Workers in uniforms moving efficiently, animals feeding in structured pens. The sun moves across the sky casting dynamic shadows. Modern infrastructure, clean lines, feelings of productivity and order. High-end corporate video aesthetic.
